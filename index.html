Vegetable Tanned
A 6 Week Process

Our leather starts in a Tannery established in 1867. Using the same environmentally friendly vegetable tanning process that has remained virtually unchanged for over 100 years it takes 6 weeks from start to the finished leather. The result...some of the finest leather you will ever touch, feel, and wear.

Leather That Gets Better
With Age

Vegetable tanned leather develops a patina over time that develops character with age. The more you wear a Hanks belt the richer the look. No other leather compares.

USA Made Leather
USA Amish Craftsmanship

Since Hanks Belts start with the best USA Vegetable tanned leathers we decided only the best craftmanship would do. Hand crafted by the Pennsylvania Amish with techniques passed down through generations. Pride and passion goes into every Hanks Belt.
